QUICK START
───────────
1. Open index.html in Chrome (double-click or drag into browser)
2. Click "🔑 No Key — Click to Add" in the top right
3. Paste your Gemini API key (starts with AIza...)
4. Go to REPORTS tab → fill in your info → select bureau → paste report
5. Click ANALYZE → generate dispute letters

GET YOUR GEMINI API KEY
────────────────────────
1. Go to: https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey
2. Click "Create API Key"
3. Copy the key (starts with AIza...)
4. Paste it into the app when prompted

Your key is stored ONLY in your browser's localStorage.
It never leaves your device or gets sent anywhere except
directly to Google's API servers.

LEGAL STRATEGIES AVAILABLE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

§609  Method of Verification Demand
      Forces bureau to disclose exact verification method.
      Bureau often can't produce → triggers deletion.

§611  Reinvestigation Demand
      Starts 30-day clock. Miss deadline = mandatory delete.
      Best all-purpose dispute strategy.

§623  Direct Furnisher Dispute
      Bypasses bureau, hits original creditor directly.
      Creates independent legal duty to investigate.
      Use as the "2" in the 1-2 Punch.

Goodwill  Deletion Request
      For accurate but isolated negative items.
      Effective with original creditors for 1-2 late payments.

FDCPA §809  Debt Validation
      Forces collectors to cease ALL activity until validated.
      Demand chain of title, original agreement, itemized balance.

§604  Permissible Purpose Challenge
      For unauthorized hard inquiries.
      Demand documentation of permissible purpose.

§605  Obsolescence / Time-Barred
      Items past 7-year DOFD limit must delete immediately.
      Re-aging is its own FCRA violation.

Pay-for-Delete
      Conditional payment offer in exchange for deletion.
      ALWAYS get written agreement BEFORE payment.
      Gray area but legally enforceable as a private contract.

HOW TO SEND DISPUTE LETTERS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. Copy the letter from the app
2. Paste into Word, Google Docs, or any word processor
3. Add [items in brackets] that need your personal details
4. Print and sign physically
5. Send via USPS Certified Mail with Return Receipt Requested
   (not email, not fax, not their online portals)
6. Keep the green return receipt card — that receipt date
   starts the bureau's 30-day statutory clock
7. Simultaneously file a CFPB complaint at:
   consumerfinance.gov/complaint

KEY RULE: Certified mail + return receipt = proof they received it.
Without this, they can claim they never got your dispute.
